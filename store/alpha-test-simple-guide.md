# Levellio Alpha — Simple Tester Guide (no tech needed)

> For our Haiti tester (doctor + artist). You don't need GitHub, code, or anything technical.
> Just use the app on your phone and tell us what you see. Your eye for **how it looks and feels**,
> and your medical read on the **Haiti health projects**, are exactly what we need.

## 1. Get the app (one time)
1. Install **Expo Go** (App Store / Google Play).
2. The host will send you a **QR code** (or a link). Open it with Expo Go to launch Levellio.
3. If the host sends a new version, just **close and reopen** the app and scan again.

## 2. What to look at (play for ~15–20 min)
Use the app like a real user and pay attention to **3 simple things**:

1. **Does it work?** — Tap things. Do buttons respond? Does anything freeze, go blank, or crash?
2. **Does it look right?** — Text cut off, overlapping, ugly spacing, wrong colors, blurry images,
   anything that looks "off" to your artist's eye.
3. **Does it make sense?** — Could a normal person in Fort-Liberté understand it? Is anything
   confusing or worded strangely? Are the **health projects** (Malaria, Clean Water, Gardens,
   Waste, School Kits) described accurately and respectfully?

Things to try: complete a habit (watch the celebration), join the **Malaria** project with the code
the host gives you, post a message in the Feed, react to someone's post.

## 3. How to send feedback (pick whatever is easiest for you)

**Option A — Send a screenshot (easiest, recommended).**
- See something good or bad? Take a **screenshot** (or screen recording).
- As an artist, feel free to **draw/circle on the screenshot** to show what you mean.
- Send it on **WhatsApp** to the host or Clifford with one line: "this looks wrong" / "I'm stuck here".

**Option B — Quick form (no login).**
- The host will share a **Google Form link**. It has big buttons: pick the screen, say works / looks
  wrong / confusing, attach a photo, send. That's it. Works on your phone, no account needed.

**Option C — Voice note.**
- Don't feel like typing? Send a **voice note** describing what you saw. Clifford will write it up.

You do **not** need to fill in any tables or checklists — Clifford will log everything for you.

## 4. Don't worry about these (they're off on purpose)
- No notifications will pop up — that's expected.
- You can't add a photo/video **to a feed post** yet — expected.
- Nothing costs money — it's all free in this test.

## 5. Your roles (where you shine)
- 🎨 **Visual/UX:** flag anything that looks unpolished or unclear — you'll catch what we miss.
- 🩺 **Medical/community accuracy:** tell us if the Haiti health projects are described correctly
  and would make sense to people on the ground.

That's it. Tap around, send screenshots, and tell us what feels off. Thank you! 🙌
