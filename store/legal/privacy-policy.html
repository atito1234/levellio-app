Levellio — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

The short version

Levellio is local-first: your habits, quests, and progress are stored on your device by default. Some features are optional and you choose them — if you create an account to use Community Projects and the community feed, that activity is stored in our cloud backend (Google Firebase) so it can be shared with the people you collaborate with. We (Ethix Innova LLC) do not use advertising or third-party analytics SDKs, and we do not sell your data. If you use a cloud AI model, you bring your own API key — it stays in your device's secure keychain and is sent only to the AI provider you pick.

1. Who this policy covers

This policy applies to the Levellio mobile app ("the App") published by Ethix Innova LLC (principal: Antonio Joel Tito, PhD) ("we", "us"). It explains what information the App handles and how. It does not cover third-party services you separately choose to use (such as an AI provider whose API key you supply, or the app store you downloaded from), which are governed by their own policies.

2. Information the App handles

Information you create — stored on your device. Quests, habits, goals, notes, XP, levels, streaks, your chosen hero, and app settings are saved locally on your device using the operating system's standard app storage. By default this solo data is not transmitted to us.

Optional account — only if you create one. Community features require a free account. When you sign up we collect your email address and a display name (you choose it) through Google Firebase Authentication. Your password is handled by Firebase Authentication; we never see it.

Community Projects & the community feed — only if you use them. When you join or create a Community Project, post, comment, react, or follow someone, that content is stored in our cloud database (Google Firebase / Firestore) so it can be shared with other members. This can include: your display name and chosen hero look, the habit titles you choose to share to a project, your contribution counts, the text of your posts and comments, your reactions, who you follow, and your account identifier (uid). You control feed visibility with the "Share my activity" toggle on each project.

Optional location — off by default. If you turn on "Capture location & speed" in Settings, the App may attach where an activity happened to your logs and confirm when a community-project habit was done on-site. This is opt-in, requires the OS location permission, and can be turned off anytime.

AI API keys — only if you opt in. If you enable cloud AI suggestions, your own API key is stored only in your device's secure keychain and used solely to send your suggestion requests to the provider you choose. We never receive, store, log, or transmit your key.

No analytics or ads. The App does not include third-party analytics, attribution, or advertising SDKs, and does not build advertising profiles.

The app store you installed from (Apple App Store or Google Play) may collect its own download, crash, and usage metrics under its policy, outside our control.

3. How information is used

Solo data is used only to run the App on your device. Account and community data are used to operate the features you opt into: signing you in across devices, running Community Projects, and showing the community feed and your network. We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or sale, and we do not sell or share personal information with data brokers.

4. On-device vs. cloud AI

  • On-device suggestions (default). Run locally and make no network calls.
  • Bring-your-own-key cloud AI (optional). The suggestion text you submit is

sent directly from your device to the third-party provider you selected (for example, Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic) using your own key. That provider's handling of your data is governed by their policy.

5. Third parties / processors

  • Google Firebase (Authentication + Firestore) — processes your account and

community/project data so those optional features work across devices. Google acts as our data processor; its handling is governed by Google's terms.

  • Your chosen AI provider — only when you enable cloud AI with your own key.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — for app distribution and, if you allow it,

store-level diagnostics.

6. Data storage, retention & deletion

  • Solo data lives on your device — delete individual items in the App, use the

in-app Danger Zone, or uninstall to remove it.

  • Your AI key — clear it anytime in Settings.
  • Account & community data — open Settings → Account → Delete account to

permanently delete your account and remove your community + project data (memberships, contributions, posts, comments, reactions, and follows) from our cloud backend. You can also leave any individual project at any time.

We retain account and community data only while your account exists; deleting your account removes it.

7. Security

Solo data uses your operating system's standard app storage; any AI key uses the OS secure keychain. Account and community data are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) to Google Firebase. No method of electronic storage is perfectly secure; keep your device updated and protected.

8. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your rights

You can access, correct, export, or delete your data: solo data directly on your device (Section 6), and account/community data via in-app account deletion. Depending on where you live (for example, under GDPR or CCPA/CPRA) you may have additional rights; contact us (Section 11) and we will help.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be reflected by a new effective date and, where appropriate, an in-app or store notice.
