The short version

Levellio is local-first. Your habits, quests, and progress are stored on your device. We ([APP-OWNER]) do not operate a server that collects your personal data, we do not require an account, and we do not use advertising or third-party analytics SDKs. If you choose to use a cloud AI model, you bring your own API key — it stays in your device's secure keychain and is sent only to the AI provider you pick.

1. Who this policy covers

This policy applies to the Levellio mobile app ("the App") published by [APP-OWNER] ("we", "us"). It explains what information the App handles and how. It does not cover third-party services you separately choose to use (such as an AI provider whose API key you supply, or the app store you downloaded from), which are governed by their own policies.

2. Information the App handles

Information you create — stored on your device. Quests, habits, goals, notes, XP, levels, streaks, your chosen hero, and app settings. This data is saved locally on your device using the operating system's standard app storage. It is not transmitted to us.

AI API keys — only if you opt in. If you enable cloud AI suggestions, you enter your own API key for a provider you choose. The key is stored only in your device's secure keychain (via the operating system's secure storage) and is used solely to send your suggestion requests to that provider. We never receive, store, log, or transmit your key to ourselves.

No accounts. The App does not require sign-up or login, and we do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, precise location, photos, or device advertising identifiers.

No analytics or ads. The App does not include third-party analytics, attribution, or advertising SDKs, and does not build advertising profiles.

The app store you installed from (Apple App Store or Google Play) may collect its own download, crash, and usage metrics under its policy, outside our control. Operating-system crash reporting applies only if you have enabled it at the OS level.

3. How information is used

Information you create is used only to run the App on your device — to show your quests, track progress, calculate streaks and levels, and render your hero. If you opt into cloud AI, your request text is sent to your chosen provider to generate suggestions. We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or sale, and we do not sell or share personal information.

4. On-device vs. cloud AI

  • On-device suggestions (default). Run locally and make **no network

calls**. Your data does not leave the device.

  • Bring-your-own-key cloud AI (optional). If you enable it, the suggestion

text you submit is sent directly from your device to the third-party provider you selected (for example, Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic) using your own key. That provider's handling of your data is governed by their privacy policy and terms. Review them before enabling cloud AI.

5. Third parties

  • Your chosen AI provider — only when you enable cloud AI with your own key.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — for app distribution and, if you allow it,

store-level diagnostics.

We do not integrate any other third-party data processors. Note: the App's codebase contains scaffolding for optional future cloud features (account sign-in and cloud backup) that are not active and collect no data in this version. If we activate them in a future release, we will update this policy and the app store data disclosures before they go live.

6. Data storage, retention & deletion

Because your data lives on your device, you control it:

  • Delete specific items — remove individual quests or habits in the App.
  • Remove your AI key — clear it anytime in Settings; this deletes it from the

secure keychain.

  • Delete everything — uninstalling the App removes its locally stored data

from your device. (If an in-app "reset" option is provided, it clears data immediately.)

We have no server-side copy of your data to retain or delete.

7. Security

Game data uses your operating system's standard app storage; any AI key uses the OS secure keychain. No method of electronic storage is perfectly secure, but because we do not transmit or centrally store your personal data, the main risks are those affecting your own device. Keep your device updated and protected.

8. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the App collects no personal data on our servers, there is no such data for us to access or delete. If you believe a child has provided personal data through a third-party service you enabled, contact that service.

9. Your rights

We do not hold your personal data on our servers, so requests to access, correct, export, or delete are fulfilled directly by you on your device (see Section 6). Depending on where you live (for example, under GDPR or CCPA/CPRA), you may have additional rights regarding any data a third-party provider or app store processes on your behalf; direct those requests to the relevant provider. For questions, contact us (Section 11).

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves (for example, if optional cloud features are activated). Material changes will be reflected by a new effective date and, where appropriate, an in-app or store notice.
